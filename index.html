This Week's Expert Insight

Macquarie's 10bps cut on variable looks small, but it's a signal. When a non-bank cuts while majors hold or raise, they're buying volume. If you have a clean borrower at CBA 5.94% with no loyalty, Macquarie at 5.49% is a 45bps conversation worth having. On a $700k loan that's $262/month.
